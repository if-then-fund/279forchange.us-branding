About

The 279 for Change Campaign is designed to change the way things are done in Washington, DC.

Instead of voting for candidates and then spending the next 2 or 4 or 6 years hoping that they do the right things, The 279 for Change Campaign goes around the traditional “wait and see” process and places firm control of certain pieces of legislation into the hands of the American people.

Something that our Founding Fathers, and Democracy had always intended.

In 2014 only about 50 pieces of real legislation were passed — in the entire year — while over 10,000 bills, many supported by the VAST majority of Americans, languished and were ignored. This campaign is changing that.

The 279 concept and campaign was founded by Richard Greene, a former Congressional Candidate and Host of “Hollywood CLOUT!” on Air America Radio, frequent contributor to The HuffingtonPost and A Political Communication Strategist for Presidents, Prime Ministers, Governors, Senators, Congresspersons, Mayors, Royalty, Celebrities and CEOs in 45 countries around the world, and author of Words That Shook The World: 100 Years of Unforgettable Speeches and Events.

Co-founder is Political Science Professor Jeanne Zaino, former Dean of Iona College and now Professor at Iona and NYU and a frequent expert guest on Fox News Channel, MSNBC and other television and radio outlets.
